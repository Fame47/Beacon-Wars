This is the quick battle manual for Beacon Wars.

Mission Setup

  • Side: choose Blue Academy or Red Academy. The CPU becomes the opposite color.
  • Commander: changes your Fleet Commander art and command profile.
  • Commander Tactic: choose Tactical Warp or Emergency Shield.
  • Battlefield: Mars or Earth. Clicking a map loads that battle.

Movement

  • Drag from a unit’s A/base to move.
  • Green circles are legal move spaces.
  • Click the A/base without dragging to inspect or activate powers.
  • Recon Runner moves in straight lines until blocked.
